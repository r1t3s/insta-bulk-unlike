üåü1. Overview

Insta Bulk Unlike ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information when you use our service to manage Instagram liked posts.

Key Principle: We believe in data minimization and local processing. Your personal information stays on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

2.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

üö´ We Never Collect:

  • Instagram Login Credentials: Usernames, passwords, or authentication tokens
  • Personal Profile Information: Name, email, phone number, or profile data
  • Posts Content or Metadata: Images, captions, comments, or post details

  • Device Information: No device identifiers, IP addresses, or browser fingerprinting
  • Location Data: No geographical information or location tracking
  • Third-Party Data: No integration with analytics services or advertising networks

üéØ3. Data Usage

The locally stored data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

3.1 Primary Functions

  • Settings Persistence: Remember your preferred speed modes, batch sizes, and delay configurations between browser sessions
  • Safety Enforcement: Enforce daily usage limits to help prevent violations of Instagram's terms of service
  • User Experience: Provide a personalized interface that adapts to your preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Maintain session state for real-time progress monitoring

3.2 Safety Features

üõ°Ô∏è Built-in Safety Mechanisms:

Our daily usage tracking helps protect your Instagram account by:

  • Preventing excessive automation that could trigger Instagram's anti-spam measures
  • Enforcing recommended daily limits based on safe usage patterns
  • Providing visual warnings when approaching usage thresholds
  • Automatically resetting counters every 24 hours

üíæ4. Data Storage

4.1 Storage Technology

All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs:

  • chrome.storage.local: For user preferences and settings
  • chrome.storage.sync: For cross-device synchronization (if enabled by user)
  • Browser Memory: For temporary session data

4.2 Storage Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Browser-level Encryption: Chrome automatically encrypts stored data
  • User Profile Isolation: Data is isolated to your specific browser profile
  • Local-only Storage: No data transmission to external servers
  • Permission-based Access: Only our extension can access its stored data

4.3 Storage Limits

Data Type Approximate Size Maximum Size

üîí Zero Data Sharing Policy

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit any user data to third parties. Period.

5.1 No Third-Party Integrations

Our extension operates independently and does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks

5.2 Legal Compliance

Since we do not collect or store personal data externally, we have no data to share even if legally compelled to do so. All data remains on your local device under your control.
